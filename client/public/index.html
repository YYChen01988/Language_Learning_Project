Welcome to BBC Languages

Here you can explore and learn new languages! Start by choosing the language you want to learn via the menu and continue to Phrases or Quiz. Phrases will show phrases in the chosen foreign language with their translation and pronounciation. The Quiz will test your new knowledge!

Additionally, take a look at the Map to see where all the languages are being spoken over the world. This will show it on the map but also list them below. If you would like to learn words that aren't in the selection, go to Add phrase to add words of your own. Lastly, use the Google select language at the bottom to translate the site to a language of your choice!
